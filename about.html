About Poorna Tech

Poorna Tech is a short-form tech content channel focused on AI tools, prompt ideas, creative video workflows, and useful digital experiments.

This website collects the prompts and resources shared through Poorna Tech videos, making it easier for viewers to open the exact prompt and try it for themselves.

The content is created for people who enjoy learning through quick examples. Many short videos move fast, so the website acts as a simple reference library where viewers can return later, find the relevant prompt, and understand how the idea was built.

Topics may include AI image prompts, video prompt ideas, creative editing concepts, useful web tools, and beginner-friendly experiments that can be adapted for personal projects.
