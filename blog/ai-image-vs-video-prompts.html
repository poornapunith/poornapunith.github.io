AI Image vs AI Video Prompts

ChatGPT and Gemini image prompts usually focus on a single frame. Seedance 2.0 video prompts need to explain what changes over time.

Image prompts need composition

Describe pose, framing, background, lighting, texture, and style. The final result is one moment.

Video prompts need movement

Describe the action, camera path, timing, continuity, and what should stay consistent between frames.

References behave differently

A reference image can guide identity or style, while a reference video can guide rhythm, motion, and transitions.
