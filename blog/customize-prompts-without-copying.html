How to Customize Prompts Without Copying

The best way to use a prompt is to keep the structure and change the creative variables. This helps you learn while still producing an original result.

Change the subject

Replace the person, object, or main character with your own idea.

Change the setting

Move the scene to a different city, room, stage, street, season, or time of day.

Change the mood

Adjust the feeling from dramatic to calm, premium, nostalgic, playful, realistic, or cinematic.

Change one variable at a time

Small changes make it easier to see what actually improved the result.
