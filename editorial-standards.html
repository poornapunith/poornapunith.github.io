Standards

Editorial Standards

Poorna Tech publishes prompt resources as learning material, not as raw dumps of text. Each public page should help a viewer understand why a prompt works, what can be changed, and which tool or output type it is best suited for.

Selection

Resources are selected when they are reusable, explainable, and useful for AI image or AI video creation. We avoid publishing pages that depend only on shock value, explicit content, or unsafe instructions.

Editing

Titles and notes are edited for clarity. Prompt documents may contain longer working text, but public pages focus on learning context, customization ideas, and responsible use.

Corrections

If a link stops working, a prompt needs clearer context, or a resource should be corrected, visitors can contact Poorna Tech through the official social channels listed on the Contact page.
