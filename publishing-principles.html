Publishing Principles

Poorna Tech is built around practical learning. A page should help creators understand a workflow, not only send them to an external document.

1. On-site value first

Every important prompt should include on-site explanation: tool type, output type, use case, and customization notes. External documents are supporting references, not the only content.

2. Adaptation over copying

Public notes encourage viewers to rewrite prompts for their own subject, style, and workflow. Copying a prompt exactly is less useful than understanding its structure.

3. Responsible framing

Pages avoid explicit, harmful, or overly graphic framing. Cinematic and action-oriented prompts are described through camera, lighting, motion, and composition rather than unsafe detail.
