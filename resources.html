Prompt and Creator Resources

This page explains the types of resources shared through Poorna Tech. The goal is to help viewers understand what each prompt category is for and how to use it responsibly.

AI image prompts

These prompts are designed for visual outputs such as portraits, character concepts, stylized scenes, posters, thumbnails, and creative edits. They often include details about subject, lighting, camera style, background, emotion, and visual finish.

AI video prompts

Video prompts focus on movement, scene direction, pacing, camera motion, and atmosphere. They are useful when a creator wants to turn a simple idea into a cinematic or social-media-ready visual moment.

Creative editing ideas

Some resources are built around editing concepts rather than a single AI tool. These may include transformation ideas, before-and- after concepts, character comparisons, or storytelling formats.

Public tools and references

Occasionally, Poorna Tech shares useful public tools, official resources, or websites that help creators experiment. These links are included when they add value beyond the video itself.
