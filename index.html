About

Practical AI prompts for everyday creators

Poorna Tech shares short-form videos focused on AI tools, creative editing, prompt writing, and useful digital workflows. Each video is paired with a prompt or resource so viewers can try the idea quickly.

The goal is to make short tutorials easier to use after watching. Instead of pausing a video and typing everything manually, visitors can open a related prompt document, study the structure, and modify it for their own image, video, or productivity experiments.

New resources are added as new Poorna Tech videos are published. The collection is designed for learners, creators, students, and anyone exploring AI-assisted content creation.
